Metric Guide

FID — Fréchet Inception Distance measures distribution distance between real and fake images. Lower = better quality & diversity.
SSIM — Structural Similarity compares luminance, contrast and structure. Score of 1.0 = perfect structural match.
PSNR — Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio measures pixel-level fidelity in dB. Higher = less noise distortion.
